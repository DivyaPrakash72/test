01-RRR

"RRR" is an upcoming Indian historical action film directed by S.S. Rajamouli. Set in the 1920s, the movie revolves around the fictional story of India's freedom fighters Alluri Sitarama Raju and Komaram Bheem, played by Ram Charan and NTR Jr. The film promises a grand cinematic experience with its compelling narrative and impressive visuals.
